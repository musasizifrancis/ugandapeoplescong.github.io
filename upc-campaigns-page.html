UPC CAMPAIGNS

Join the Uganda People's Congress in our nationwide efforts to build a better future for all Ugandans. Discover our current campaigns and learn how you can get involved.

ACTIVE CAMPAIGNS

Launch Date: February 15, 2025 Duration: 9 months

Economic Recovery Initiative

Our flagship campaign focuses on developing policies and grassroots initiatives to combat inflation, create jobs, and rebuild Uganda's economic foundation through sustainable development.

The initiative includes town halls across all regions to gather citizen input on economic priorities.

Launch Date: January 10, 2025 Duration: 12 months

Youth Empowerment Program

Engaging Uganda's youth through leadership development, skills training, and political participation. This nationwide program focuses on building the next generation of Ugandan leaders.

Includes monthly workshops, mentorship opportunities, and youth forums in all major universities.

Launch Date: March 5, 2025 Duration: 6 months

Healthcare Access Campaign

Advocating for universal healthcare coverage and improved medical facilities in rural communities. Our campaign addresses the critical gaps in Uganda's healthcare system.

Includes community health drives and policy development for national healthcare reform.

REGIONAL FOCUS AREAS

Northern Uganda

Post-conflict reconstruction and development initiatives focusing on infrastructure, education, and economic opportunities.

Eastern Uganda

Agricultural modernization programs and cross-border trade development with Kenya and South Sudan.

Western Uganda

Environmental conservation, tourism development, and sustainable exploitation of natural resources.

Central Uganda

Urban development, industrial growth, and addressing challenges of rapid urbanization in the capital region.

ISSUE-BASED CAMPAIGNS

Education Reform

Advocating for quality education accessible to all Ugandans regardless of socioeconomic status. Our campaign pushes for curriculum modernization, teacher training, and increased education funding.

Key goals include reducing the teacher-student ratio and implementing technology in rural schools.

READ POLICY

Agricultural Transformation

Supporting Uganda's farmers through modern farming techniques, access to markets, and fair pricing policies. Our campaign aims to transform Uganda's agricultural sector into a powerful economic driver.

Initiatives include cooperative development and agricultural finance programs.

READ POLICY

Democratic Governance

Championing transparency, accountability, and civic participation in governance. UPC advocates for strengthening democratic institutions and protecting civil liberties.

This includes voter education programs and election monitoring initiatives.

READ POLICY

Infrastructure Development

Campaigning for critical investments in roads, energy, water, and telecommunications to connect all Ugandans to essential services and economic opportunities.

Our focus includes rural electrification and water access programs.
